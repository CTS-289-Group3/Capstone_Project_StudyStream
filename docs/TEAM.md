## 🛠️ Team Contributions

All team members collaborated across the full development stack of the StudyStream project. Responsibilities were shared rather than strictly divided, with each member contributing to:

- **Backend Development (Django)**  
- **Frontend / UI Design**  
- **Database Design and Model Implementation**  
- **Testing and Debugging**  

Work was completed collaboratively, with team members supporting each other across all areas to ensure consistency, functionality, and integration throughout the application.

---

## 🤝 Collaboration Approach

The team followed a collaborative workflow focused on shared problem-solving and continuous improvement. This approach allowed for strong integration between components and a cohesive final product.

---

## 🚀 Project Focus

StudyStream was developed as a hybrid productivity system combining structured digital planning with real-world visibility. The team focused on:

- Breaking down complex assignments into manageable tasks  
- Visualizing workload across time  
- Integrating academic, personal, and work schedules into one unified system
